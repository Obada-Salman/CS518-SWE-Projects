Installation:
  $ cd /home/sawda/CS518/CS-371-518-Software-Engineering-Projects/vegetable_war
  $ pip install -r requirements.txt

Testing Installation:
  $ python3 test_imports.py

Running the Game:
  $ python3 onions_may_cry.py
  OR
  $ ./run.sh
  OR
  $ cd src && python3 main.py

Playing:
  - Press SPACE to start from menu
  - Follow on-screen instructions
  - Use A/D to move, W to jump, SPACE to attack
  - Press SHIFT for super attack when emotion meter is full
  - Defeat all enemies in each stage to progress

================================================================================
                            GAME CONTROLS
================================================================================

Movement:
  A/D          - Move left/right
  W            - Jump
  
Combat:
  SPACE        - Basic attack (15 damage)
  SHIFT        - Super attack (50 damage, requires full emotion meter)
  
Menu:
  SPACE        - Advance text/continue
  ESC          - Quit game

================================================================================
                            GAME STAGES
================================================================================

Stage 1: The Bean Invasion
  - Enemies: Brown Beans (slow, 30 HP, 10 damage)
  - Challenge: Learn combat basics
  - Duration: 2-3 minutes
  - Difficulty: ⭐ Easy

Stage 2: The Pea Swarm
  - Enemies: Green Peas (fast, 20 HP, 8 damage)
  - Challenge: Moving platforms + fast enemies
  - Duration: 3-4 minutes
  - Difficulty: ⭐⭐ Medium

Stage 3: The Garlic Conspiracy
  - Boss: Giant Garlic (150 HP, 20 damage, summons minions)
  - Challenge: Epic boss fight with patterns
  - Duration: 5-10 minutes
  - Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐ Hard

================================================================================
                            GAME MECHANICS
================================================================================

Emotion Meter:
  - Fills with 8 points per basic hit
  - Fills with 25 points per enemy defeated
  - Reaches maximum at 100 points
  - Enables super attack (50 damage, large area)
  - Resets to 0 when super attack is used

Combat:
  - Basic Attack: 15 damage per hit
  - Super Attack: 50 damage in large area
  - Knockback: Enemies pushed away by hits
  - Invincibility: Brief protection after damage

Enemy Mechanics:
  - Patrol behavior between random positions
  - Attack when player in range
  - Different speeds and health values
  - Boss summons minions

Platform Mechanics:
  - Static platforms you stand on
  - Moving platforms that oscillate
  - Interactive platforms (future expansion)

Physics:
  - Gravity: 0.6 units/frame
  - Jump strength: -15 units/frame
  - Movement speed: 5 units/frame
  - Max fall speed: 20 units/frame
