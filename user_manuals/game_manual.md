# Onions May Cry User Manual

## Overview
Onions May Cry is a side-scrolling action game where you move through story levels, defeat enemies, collect resources, and reach the goal door to progress.

## Main Menu
From the main menu, you can:
- Enter your player name.
- Start Story Mode.
- Open Settings.
- Open the Level Builder.
- Quit the game.

## Basic Controls
- Left Arrow: move left.
- Right Arrow: move right.
- Up Arrow or Space: jump.
- X: shoot a tear attack.
- Escape: pause the game.
- E: recruit a nearby ally in story levels.

## Story Mode
Story Mode is the main campaign.

### How to Play
1. Choose Story Mode from the main menu.
2. Select an available stage.
3. Defeat enemies and collect resources.
4. Reach the door after the stage is cleared.

### Gameplay Notes
- The door stays locked until all enemies are defeated.
- Your health is shown at the top-left of the screen.
- Tears can damage enemies from a distance.
- Collectible resources include water, sunlight, and nutrients.
- Allies can be recruited when you are close enough and press E.

## Pause Menu
Press Escape during Story Mode to pause. From the pause screen, you can:
- Resume the game.
- Go to Level Select.
- Return to the Main Menu.

## Settings
The Settings screen lets you change the display size or switch to fullscreen.

Available options:
- 800x600
- 1280x720
- 1920x1080
- Fullscreen

## Level Builder
The Level Builder lets you create or edit maps.

### Controls
- Left click a tile to place it.
- Right click to remove a tile.
- A and D to scroll across the map.
- Left and Right Arrow keys to change the level number.
- Click the level type button to switch between community and story maps.

### Saving
You can only save a level if both a player spawn and a goal tile have been placed.

## Leaderboard
The game includes an online leaderboard.

- Your player name is used when scores are submitted.
- Scores are tracked with completion time and level name.
- The leaderboard server can display the top scores in a browser.

## Tips
- Keep moving to avoid enemy damage.
- Use jumps carefully to cross gaps and platforms.
- Shoot enemies from a safe distance when possible.
- Save custom levels only after placing both the player and goal.
